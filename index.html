More about me

As a fast and eager learning autodidact I have been in touch with many different aspects when it comes to graphics design and what is required on the back end to make it functional.

My area of focus is webdesign and development and a game project (A sort of city builder) in my spare time — however my interest does not stop there and can be found in almost everything related to computers and graphics.

It all started out with a school project back in 1998 where I had to create a website (With super awesome flashy GIF's!, because that is how it was done), since then I have barely left my computer alone. From creating websites with HTML and Photoshop in the beginning, then wanting to learn more techniques grabbing 3D illustration/ animation with it a few years later. Wanting my projects to work and not only look pretty I started reading up on scripting/ programming too which now all come together.
